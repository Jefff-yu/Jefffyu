可以了

可以了

互動裝置 / 50*50*1.5cm / 2021
木材、Arduino、馬達、距離感測器

Interactive Installation / 50*50*1.5cm / 2021
Wood, Arduino, Motors, Distance Sensor

「真正的圓形只存在於數學定義之中。」
"The true circle only exists in mathematical definitions."

 完美主義者不斷追求不現實的目標，如試圖接近這個圓。而驅使自己達成不現實的目標常常會帶來罪惡感、自卑感以及焦慮。也如物極必反的道理，追求到達到極致完美，往往招致痛苦。 我們以這件作品再現這個過程，提醒觀也提醒創作者本身是否過於鑽牛角尖，適當學習接受不完美的好，告訴自己，可以了。
Perfectionists constantly pursue unrealistic goals, attempting to get closer to this ideal circle. However, striving for unreal perfection often brings about feelings of guilt, inferiority, and anxiety. As the saying goes, the pursuit of extreme perfection often leads to suffering.
We recreate this process with this artwork, reminding both the audience and the creators themselves whether they are getting too caught up in trivial details. It encourages learning to accept imperfection appropriately and reassuring oneself, saying, "Okay, it's Fine."

游覺輔、商睿庭、陳彥霖
