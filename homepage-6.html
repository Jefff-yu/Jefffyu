家庭樣本

家庭樣本

互動裝置/依場地而定/2024年
木材、Esp32s、微型電腦、3D列印、馬達、螢幕

Interactive device / site-specific / 2024
Wood, ESP32s, microcomputers, 3D printing, motors, screens

當今社會對家庭的樣貌已經有更多元的認識，但我們還是可能在不知不覺中陷入「對理想家庭關係的美好想像」之中。然而「家庭」的結構並非本質性的，而是建構式、透過實踐堆疊而成的。「家庭樣本」這件作品模擬我們腦中「強迫性的美好家庭想像」，將工廠中對產品品質控管的邏輯套用在動態的家庭關係上，從反諷的視角提醒觀眾「沒有完美的家庭，家庭也沒有一定要怎樣」，並且放下對於家庭關係「應該」和「應當」的期待。

In today's society, there is a more diverse understanding of the concept of family. However, we may still unconsciously fall into the trap of "idealized family relationships." Yet, the structure of "family" is not inherent; rather, it is constructed through practice and accumulation. "Family Samples" simulates the compulsive idealized family image in our minds, applying the logic of product quality control in factories to dynamic family relationships. From a satirical perspective, it reminds the audience that "there is no perfect family, and families don't have to be a certain way," urging them to let go of expectations about how family relationships "should" or "must" be.

游覺輔、賴玟霓
