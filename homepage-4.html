「隨浪花昏沉 」發想自從新竹回到家鄉台東後的經歷，原以為去看海可以讓人放鬆身心， 結果因為還有事情沒有做完，去到的海岸也並非想像中的沙灘而是礫灘。海浪聲沒有撫慰我。 我反而注視著濺起的浪花，混雜著回到家的快樂，和一些難以言說的惆悵與慵懶的氣息。

/

"Tossed Amidst the Waves"

The inspiration for "Tossed Amidst the Waves" stems from my experiences returning to my hometown, Taitung, after being in Hsinchu. Initially, I thought a visit to the sea would bring relaxation to my mind and body. However, the reality differed as there were unfinished tasks, and the coastal area I arrived at was not the sandy beach I had imagined, but rather a pebble shore. The sound of the waves did not soothe me. Instead, I found myself fixated on the splashing seafoam, a mix of the joy of returning home and an indescribable sense of melancholy and languor.
